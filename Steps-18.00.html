Step 10 - Patients' Sat score by waiting time group 18


 

 

Here are the concise steps to create the "Patient Sat Score by WaitTimeGroup" chart:

  • Insert a Clustered Column and Line Chart:
    • From the Visualizations pane, select the Clustered Column and Line Chart.
  • Set Up the Axis and Values:
    • Drag the WaitTimeGroup field to the Shared Axis area.
    • Drag the Average of AdjustedPatientSatScore field to the Column Values area.
    • Drag the Satisfaction Rate field to the Line Values area.
  • Configure Dual Y-Axes:
    • Ensure one Y-Axis shows the Average of AdjustedPatientSatScore and the other shows the Satisfaction Rate percentage.
  • Title and Formatting:
    • Set the chart title to "Patient Sat Score by WaitTimeGroup."
