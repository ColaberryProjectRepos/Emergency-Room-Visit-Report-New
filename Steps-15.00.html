Step 6 - Create a Year Slider 15

 

  • Add a Slicer Visual:
    • From the Visualizations pane, select the Slicer Visual.
  • Select the Year Value:
    • In the Fields pane, expand the "Main Dataset".
    • Drag the Date column to the slicer field.
    • Change the slicer format to show only the Year by selecting the slicer and choosing the "Between" option from the Slicer Settings under the Format pane -> Set Size = Height = 87, Width = 172
  • Format the Slicer:
    • Adjust the slicer formatting to match the desired look as shown in the provided GIF.
