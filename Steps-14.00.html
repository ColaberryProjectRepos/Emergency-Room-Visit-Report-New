Step 5 - Adding Logos, Title, and Background 14

Colaberry Logo:

  • Insert: Power BI Desktop ribbon ? Insert ? Image ? Select Colaberry logo.
  • Adjust Size & Position: Visualizations pane ? General ? Set Size: Height=73, Width=227; Position: Horizontal=0, Vertical=7.

 

Title: "Emergency Room Visit Report":

  • Insert: Power BI Desktop ribbon ? Insert ? Text box ? Type 'Emergency Room Visit Report'.
  • Format Text: Center alignment, Font: Segoe (Bold), Size: 28, Color: Black)
  • Adjust Size & Position: General ? Size: Adjust according to visual preference; Position the title appropriately across the top.
  • Add Icon : Insert icon next to the title using Image or Shape from the Insert menu, then position it to the right of the text.
  • Send to Back: Format ? Send Backward ? Send to Back (if overlapping with other visuals).

 

Background:

  • Insert & Adjust:
    1. Go to the Power BI Desktop ribbon ? Insert ? Image ? Select the desired background image.
    2. Adjust the transparency of the image to 90% by selecting the image, navigating to the Format pane, and setting the Transparency under the Image settings to 90%
